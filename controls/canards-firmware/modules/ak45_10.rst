AK45-10 (Servo)
===============

Assignee: Michael

- Low-level driver for the Cubemars AK45-10.
- Likely use *position loop* or *position-velocity loop* mode.
- Position is 32-bit integer; speed and acceleration are 16-bit integers.

Links
-----
- `Upper Computer & datasheet downloads <https://www.cubemars.com/technical-support-and-software-download.html>`_

Notes
-----
- Run identification & encoder verification in the vendor tool (no load), then write parameters.
- Driver LEDs: Blue=power, Green=operation, Red=fault. On power, green & red briefly light (~2 s).
- Example error codes: 1=over-temp, 2=overcurrent, 3=overvoltage, 5=encoder fault.
